About College

Amar Singh College (امر سنگھ کالج سرینگر, Kashmiri: اَمرسنگھ ژاٹٕھل سِرینگر) is an academic & professional college in Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir, India. Established in November 1913, it is the second oldest college in the Kashmir Valley after Sri Pratap College. The College was recognized by University Grants Commission of India (UGC) in June 1972. The College is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council [NAAC] with B++ Grade. The college is located in Wazir Bagh, in the city of Srinagar. It is spread over 35 hectares of land divided into a large play ground, parks, gardens, and an infrastructural area. At present, the college has multiple academic buildings making a total built-up area of about 30000 sqm. The college offers Undergraduate Honours Programs in 25 Majors including Commerce, Political Science, English, Sociology, Education, Zoology, Psychology, Economics, History, Computer Applications, Urdu, Tourism and Travel Management, Geography, Botany, EVS, Mass Communication, Islamic Studies, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, Arabic, Geology, Philosophy, Statistics, Kashmiri. The college also offers some courses as Minor only, like Bioresources, Functional English, Public Administration, Anthropology, Music and Persian. The college also offers Postgraduate programs in Economics, Geography and English

Vision & Mission

Vision Statement At Amar Singh College, our vision is to be a center of excellence in higher education that fosters intellectual growth, critical thinking, and a lifelong passion for learning. We aspire to empower our students to become responsible citizens, ethical leaders, and contributors to the global knowledge society while preserving cultural heritage and promoting sustainable development. Mission Statement

  • Holistic Development: To nurture the physical, emotional, and intellectual growth of students by offering a dynamic environment rich in co-curricular and extracurricular opportunities.
  • Community Engagement: To instill a strong sense of social responsibility through community service, environmental awareness, and outreach programs.
  • Faculty Empowerment: To promote continuous professional development and research among faculty, ensuring they remain leaders in their disciplines.
  • Cultural Integrity & Ethics: To uphold the values of honesty, inclusiveness, tolerance, and cultural respect in all aspects of campus life.
  • Innovation & Sustainability: To integrate modern technologies and sustainable practices into teaching, learning, and campus development.
  • By staying true to this mission, Amar Singh College aims to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable but also socially conscious and prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving world.

Our Legacy

Founded over a century ago in 1913, Amar Singh College stands as a beacon of knowledge and heritage in the Kashmir Valley. Named after Maharaja Amar Singh, the college was originally established as an intermediate college and has since evolved into a prestigious institution of higher learning. With a legacy rooted in academic excellence, Amar Singh College is the second oldest college in the region and has contributed significantly to shaping the intellectual and cultural landscape of Jammu & Kashmir. It has nurtured generations of scholars, civil servants, educators, and professionals who have gone on to make meaningful contributions both nationally and internationally. The college has not only withstood the test of time but has also thrived through decades of social, political, and educational transformations. Its commitment to inclusive, progressive, and holistic education remains steadfast. From its historic colonial architecture to its modern academic initiatives, Amar Singh College symbolizes a harmonious blend of the past and future—a legacy that continues to inspire excellence, discipline, and intellectual curiosity.
