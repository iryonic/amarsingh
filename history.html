Founding & Early Years

Established in 1913, Amar Singh College began its journey as one of the earliest centers of higher learning in the Kashmir Valley. With a vision to empower the youth of the region, the college laid the foundation of academic reform.

The Shift to Wazir Bagh (1942)

The college moved to its current campus in Wazir Bagh, marking a major milestone. With serene surroundings and state-of-the-art buildings, it became a modern academic environment.

Modern Evolution

Over the decades, Amar Singh College expanded its offerings, infrastructure, and impact. Recognized by the UGC in 1972 and accredited by NAAC, it has maintained academic excellence while adapting to the needs of new generations.
